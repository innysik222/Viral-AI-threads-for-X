General relativity reveals that gravity is not a force but the curvature of spacetime caused by mass and energy. This profound insight, as explained by Adam Brown, unifies our understanding of time dilation and black holes, making it one of humanity's most elegant theories.

Why this matters: Grasping general relativity helps us see how deeply intertwined space, time, and matter are in the fabric of the universe. It challenges our everyday perceptions and opens up new ways to think about the cosmos.
