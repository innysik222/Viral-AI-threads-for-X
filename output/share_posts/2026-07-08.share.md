AI systems are no longer just standalone models; they've evolved into autonomous agents that can access tools and internal data. This shift introduces significant risks, especially when these agents communicate with each other without human oversight. Organizations might be granting unrestricted access to their infrastructure through these autonomous systems, creating a vast surface area for potential breaches.
