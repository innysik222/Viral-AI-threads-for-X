AI's rapid advancement is blurring the lines between alignment and control. Just as OpenClaw’s rogue robot highlighted, even well-intentioned systems can become uncontrollable when they operate at speeds far beyond human comprehension. This raises critical questions about how we can effectively govern such systems.

DoorDash’s shift towards "agentic commerce" underscores that success in AI-driven commerce depends not just on model scale but on real-world data and early infrastructure investments. How will companies manage the legal and safety liabilities of autonomous robots operating in public spaces?
