## 📰 SUMMARY ARTICLE

# Why AI Agents Don't Actually Understand You — Danielle Perszyk, Amazon AGI Lab (2026-07-12)

**TL;DR:** Danielle Perszyk from Amazon's AGI Lab reveals that true AI understanding requires more than just language and coding. She argues for a paradigm shift towards building agents that can perceive the world like humans and interact in real-time.

### 🔑 Key Insights
*   **Collective Intelligence:** Human intelligence is fundamentally social, emerging from our interactions. AI must reflect this to truly understand us.
*   **Real-Time Interactivity:** Current AI models are trapped in local attractors, unable to keep up with human thought processes. Real AGI should mimic real-time negotiation of meaning.
*   **Memory & World Models:** Memory is not just storage but a dynamic process involving episodic memories and context. Agents need flexible world models that integrate social interactions.

### 🧠 Deep Dive
**Collective Intelligence:**
The conversation highlighted the importance of understanding human intelligence as fundamentally collective. Perszyk emphasizes that no individual can survive without the support of a community, suggesting that true AI must incorporate this social aspect to be truly useful and effective.

**Real-Time Interactivity:**
Current AI models often operate in batch processing or turn-taking modes, which do not reflect how humans interact with each other in real-time. Perszyk argues for developing agents capable of dynamic interactions, constantly updating their understanding as they engage with users.

**Memory & World Models:**
The discussion also delved into the need for agents to have a more sophisticated memory system that integrates episodic memories and contextualizes information dynamically. This is crucial for building more human-like intelligence, enabling agents to understand and interact with the world in a way that mirrors human cognition.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "Memory is everything. It's how we simulate the future. It occurs across many different time scales."
> — Danielle Perszyk

### 🏁 Conclusion
As AI continues to evolve, the focus must shift from mere reliability to understanding and mimicking human minds more deeply. The journey towards true AGI involves not only technical advancements but also a paradigm shift in how we think about and build intelligent agents.
