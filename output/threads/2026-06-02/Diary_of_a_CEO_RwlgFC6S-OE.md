*   Robots will replace manual jobs by 2030.

### 🧠 Deep Dive

**The Coming Revolution:** Mo Gawdat warns that within just 3 years, artificial general intelligence (AGI) is set to disrupt the job market. By 2030, robots will replace manual labor across various sectors. This rapid shift necessitates a reevaluation of skills and education.

**Ethics in AI:** As superintelligence approaches, the ethical considerations become paramount. Gawdat emphasizes that ethical AI should prioritize human well-being over profit-driven algorithms. He argues that governments need to intervene to ensure tech companies adopt ethical practices.

**Prepare for Change:** To thrive in this new era, individuals must adapt. Gawdat suggests embracing hybrid work models where humans and AI collaborate effectively. At the same time, investing in human skills that cannot be automated is crucial. Remaining informed about AI developments and their implications is also essential to avoid being misled by hype.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "We will end up in a utopia of abundance."

### 🏁 Conclusion
The future is near, and it demands our attention. As AGI looms on the horizon, ethical considerations must guide its development. By preparing now and advocating for ethical practices, we can ensure a better future.
