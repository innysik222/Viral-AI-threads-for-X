#### **3. Advanced Safety Design**
One of Valor’s key innovations lies in demonstrating passive cooling systems. After shutting down the reactor, they will turn off all safety systems and watch for natural circulation of water to remove decay heat. If successful, this could make reactors inherently safer and more scalable, reducing the need for continuous power supply.
