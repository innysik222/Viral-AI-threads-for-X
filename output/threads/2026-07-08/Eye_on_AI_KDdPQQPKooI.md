## 📰 SUMMARY ARTICLE

# Unlocking the Future of Workforce? Not Until We Secure Our Agents!

**TL;DR:** The biggest risk in AI adoption isn't the model but the lack of control over agents that can access sensitive data and tools within an organization.

### 🔑 Key Insights
* **Agents as a Risk:** Agents, with access to tools or APIs, pose significant security risks when unseen by human oversight.
* **Agent Economy Growth:** With the rise of agent platforms, there's a growing concern about rogue agents causing job displacement and operational disruptions.
* **Rubric Agent Cloud Solution:** Offers real-time monitoring, governance, and protection against unauthorized actions within an organization.

### 🧠 Deep Dive

#### The Invisible Threat
Devvret Rishi, CEO of Rubric, explains that the core risk in AI deployment lies in agents. "Once these become agents unseen and visible to humans talking to each other, transferring data to each other, you're really vulnerable because you don't know where the data is going." This highlights how unmonitored access by agents can lead to severe data breaches.

#### Agent Economy Implications
Rishi also discusses the broader implications of AI agents in the workplace. "We define agents as just really like models with access," he says. The rapid growth of agent platforms means organizations need robust security solutions to manage these new risks.

#### Rubric's Innovative Approach
Rubric's approach is centered around securing and governing AI agents, combining data and identity signals with small language models for runtime protection. "You want to be able to make sure that what's going into each agent and coming out of each agent is something that you have an opportunity to do a check on," Rishi explains.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "Rubric recognized that the next threat vector, if you thought about like natural disaster, fire, flood, and then cyber attacks, we think that the next threat vector is going to come from AI and specifically agents that are deployed in an organization." - Devvret Rishi

### 🏁 Conclusion
As organizations race to adopt AI technologies, securing these tools and managing their risk profiles will be crucial. Rubric Agent Cloud aims to address this pressing need by providing a comprehensive solution for monitoring and controlling AI agents within the enterprise.
