## 📰 SUMMARY ARTICLE

# Every Product Will Be A Living System: The Future Of AI According To Trajectory.ai

**TL;DR:** Trajectory.ai, co-founded by Ronak Malde, is at the forefront of continual learning, transforming industries from coding to legal with a platform that enables real-world usage and dynamic model updates.

### 🔑 Key Insights
*   **Continual Learning**: The core thesis behind Trajectory.ai is that every product will need to be continually updated based on user interactions.
*   **Self-Distillation Policy Optimization (SDPO)**: A new algorithm that accelerates the training of models by using real-world data more effectively.
*   **Customer-Centric Approach**: Trajectory.ai aims to make continual learning accessible and controllable for end-users, rather than just researchers.

### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### Continual Learning as a Paradigm Shift
Ronak Malde from Trajectory.ai emphasizes that the future of AI lies in continually updating models based on real-world user interactions. This shift moves away from static models to intelligent systems that grow and adapt with use. The company's platform takes data from various domains, such as coding, legal workflows, and healthcare, and uses it to train models that can be continuously improved.

#### Self-Distillation Policy Optimization (SDPO)
One of the key technologies Trajectory.ai is developing is SDPO. This algorithm helps in scaling up self-distillation, a process where a student model learns from a teacher model by distilling its knowledge. By using privileged information and hints, SDPO can train models faster and more effectively, making it easier to apply continual learning in real-world scenarios.

#### Customer-Centric Innovation
Trajectory.ai's approach is not just about technology but also about user experience. The company aims to make sure that the intelligence of products grows dynamically based on how users interact with them. This means providing tools and interfaces for product managers to understand where their agents are performing well and where they need improvement, allowing continuous optimization.

#### Real-World Applications
The platform has already seen success in industries like legal services, where Trajectory.ai's model improved metrics such as issue spotting, analysis, and explanation across various workflows. The company is now expanding its reach to Fortune 500 companies, aiming to apply continual learning principles to transform how these large enterprises operate.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "The next phase is one I like it kind of what we think of as act two of the company is I want to make this legible to the customer. So just like the right observability tools, the right abstraction levels for evals and then second give them control." - Ronak Malde

### 🏁 Conclusion
Trajectory.ai's vision is transformative. By leveraging continual learning and SDPO, they are setting a new standard for how AI will be integrated into every product of the future. This approach not only enhances user experience but also paves the way for more intelligent and adaptable systems.
