### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### The Core of Evolutionary AI

Evolutionary AI is a population-based method that uses principles from natural evolution. Unlike gradient descent methods, which follow small, incremental changes towards an optimal solution, evolutionary algorithms explore the search space more broadly and make larger jumps by recombining different solutions. This diversity allows for surprising discoveries that might not be found through conventional optimization techniques.

#### Practical Applications

Risto Miikkulainen highlights practical applications of evolutionary AI in diverse fields:
- **Stock Trading:** Using evolutionary methods to find unique trading strategies.
- **Neuroevolution:** Evolving neural network architectures and decision-making systems that can adapt and learn continuously.
- **Marketing & Research:** Applying evolutionary approaches to budget allocation, clinical trials, and marketing strategies.
