## 📰 SUMMARY ARTICLE

# The Data Gap: Why AI in Business is Failing and How to Fix It

**TL;DR:** According to a survey of 850 data leaders, most issues with AI agents are not due to model shortcomings but rather data access and consistency. Denodo's semantic layer offers a solution by enabling real-time data querying from multiple sources.

### 🔑 Key Insights
*   **Data Access Issues**: Over 70% of organizations report problems related to the lack of real-time data.
*   **Multiple Data Sources Needed**: On average, AI agents require data from around 400 different sources.
*   **Consistent Semantics**: Inconsistencies in how data is defined and used across different systems contribute significantly to failures.

### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### The Trust Gap: Root Causes

Alberto Pan, CTO of Denodo, highlights that the primary issue with AI adoption in large enterprises isn't a lack of model intelligence. Instead, it's "mostly about data," particularly around access and semantics. "[The] failure modes today are related to data," says Pan.

#### Real-Time Data vs. Centralized Systems

Traditional data management architectures like data warehouses or lakehouses were designed for analytics, not real-time decision-making. These systems force a centralization of data, leading to latency issues that can be fatal in fast-moving business contexts. "[Data] will never be 100% real time," Pan explains.

#### Denodo's Solution

Denodo offers a semantic layer that allows querying live data from wherever it resides. This approach eliminates the need for upfront data replication and ensures up-to-date information is accessible to AI agents in real-time workflows. "With Denodo, you don't need to centralize the data up front," Pan notes.

#### Governance and Security

Another critical aspect highlighted by Pan is consistent governance across multiple data sources. "[You] have several problems" when dealing with diverse data systems, including enforcing security policies and ensuring compliance. Denodo's architecture simplifies these challenges through centralized management of rules.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "The biggest gap is not the model but getting right data and semantics," says Alberto Pan, CTO of Denodo.
>
> "[Data] will never be 100% real time" in traditional architectures, warns Pan.

### 🏁 Conclusion

For businesses looking to implement AI effectively, understanding and addressing these data-related challenges is crucial. While Denodo offers a powerful solution, the key takeaway is that organizations must prioritize data access, consistency, and governance to truly unlock AI's potential.
