# The Real World of Industrial AI: Kriti Sharma's Vision for Nexus Black

**TL;DR:** Kriti Sharma, CEO of IFS's Nexus Black unit, discusses how industrial artificial intelligence (AI) solves complex real-world problems in hardhat environments. Key insights include the importance of deep domain expertise and on-site collaboration to ensure production-grade solutions.

### 🔑 Key Insights
*   **On-Site Collaboration:** Engineers spend time on-site with field technicians to understand their challenges.
*   **High-Value Solutions:** Focus is on solving specific, high-value problems rather than generic AI solutions.
*   **Customization:** Solutions are tailored for industries like manufacturing and utilities, ensuring they fit the unique needs of each environment.

### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### On-Site Collaboration
Kriti Sharma emphasizes the critical role of being "on the ground" when developing industrial AI solutions. The team at Nexus Black works closely with field technicians to observe their workflows, understand real-world challenges, and tailor solutions accordingly. For instance, they observed that taking off safety gloves to type on devices was impractical, leading them to develop voice transcription capabilities.

#### High-Value Solutions
The primary focus of Nexus Black is on delivering high-value solutions rather than broad, generic AI applications. In a case study at the William Grant distillery, AI helped reduce production loss by predicting and fixing issues before they occurred, saving $8.4 million annually. This approach ensures that the solutions are not only effective but also directly address critical business challenges.

#### Customization
Nexus Black's success lies in its ability to provide customized solutions for specific industries such as manufacturing, utilities, and aerospace. The team collaborates closely with subject matter experts from these sectors to ensure their AI tools fit seamlessly into existing workflows and environments. For example, they developed Resolve, a product designed specifically for frontline workers in factories, which monitors systems and provides detailed instructions based on sensor data.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "The value really comes in from knowing which problems need to be solved. And it's really also no longer about the best technology or the best model. It's about using it in the best possible way to solve a hard problem." - Kriti Sharma, CEO of Nexus Black

### 🏁 Conclusion
Nexus Black's approach to industrial AI demonstrates that true innovation lies not just in cutting-edge technology but in deep domain expertise and on-site collaboration. By working closely with field technicians and subject matter experts, they ensure their solutions are practical, effective, and tailored to the unique needs of each industry.
