#### AIE Conference Evolution: Overcoming Initial Skepticism

Matthew Berman and his partner Ben faced significant challenges when they first launched the AI Engineering (AIE) conference. "The very first one you got nothing," Berman recalls. Over time, AIE has grown into a reputable event where labs like OpenAI compete on an even playing field.

#### The Next Generation in AI Chips

In the conversation, Matthew Berman discussed the future of AI hardware. "Etched and Maddx are doing much more dedicated work for post-transformer models," he explained. This new generation of chips is not meant to disrupt established players like Nvidia but rather to optimize for specific workloads that have emerged since the early days of AI.
