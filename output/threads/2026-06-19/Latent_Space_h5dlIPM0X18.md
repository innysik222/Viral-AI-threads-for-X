### 🧠 Deep Dive
**Culture Over Beliefs**
Anjney Midha highlights the importance of culture in AI labs. "Culture is not a set of beliefs; it's a set of actions," he says, emphasizing that actions like mission alignment are crucial for maintaining a strong culture.

**Infrastructure Challenges**
Midha points out that high node utilization (95%) should be standard practice to avoid resource wastage. He argues that AI labs often fall short due to misaligned incentives and inadequate infrastructure management. "There's no excuse," he states, "like I think 95% is considered an outage at Google."

**Sustainable Scaling**
To scale AI sustainably, Midha suggests two approaches: standardizing protocols or inventing new capabilities that can unlock net new capacity. He cites the example of Amp's mission to make mega-flops flow like megawatts, emphasizing the need for pooling and utilization across clouds.
