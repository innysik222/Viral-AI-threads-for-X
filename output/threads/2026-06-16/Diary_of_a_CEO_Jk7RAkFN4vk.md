# Anti-Aging Expert Reveals the Surprising Benefits of Creatine

**TL;DR:** Dr. Darren Candow, a leading anti-aging expert, reveals that creatine is not just for bodybuilders—taking this supplement can improve brain function and bone health, among other benefits.

*   **Bone Health**: For post-menopausal women, a dose of 8 to 12 grams of creatine can slow down bone mineral density loss and reduce the risk of fractures.

### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### Brain Health
Dr. Candow discusses how creatine supplementation can help maintain brain bioenergetics, particularly during times of stress. He mentions that for a healthy brain, 10 grams is sufficient, but for those under metabolic stress, such as sleep deprivation or night shift workers, higher doses may be necessary.

#### Bone Health
The expert highlights the benefits of creatine in maintaining bone health, especially in post-menopausal women. He notes that while it doesn't increase bone density, creatine can reduce the rate of bone mineral density loss around the hip region and maintain bone structure.

#### Sleep Improvement
Dr. Candow shares a study where individuals who took 20 grams of creatine before sleep showed improved quality and duration of their sleep. This finding is particularly relevant for those dealing with jet lag or irregular sleeping patterns.
