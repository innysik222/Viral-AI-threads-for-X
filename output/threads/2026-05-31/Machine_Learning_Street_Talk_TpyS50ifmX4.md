### 🧠 Deep Dive

**Rising Threats from AI Models**
The conversation around AI has shifted from potential benefits to growing concerns. Experts like Brad Carson warn that advanced models such as Mythos represent a significant threat. These models are increasingly capable of making decisions with profound consequences, raising questions about whose values will guide these choices.

**Digital Divides in the Age of AI**
Access to cutting-edge AI tools is currently concentrated among a few large companies and governments. This concentration could create new divides between those who have access to powerful technologies and those who do not. Brad Carson argues that this disparity is inevitable, with the best minds and resources flowing into private sector labs.

**Congressional Challenges in Regulating AI**
With limited time and expertise, Congress faces a daunting task in regulating AI effectively. Brad Carson suggests that creating a congressional think tank could help bridge this gap by providing policymakers with expert insights on emerging technologies.
