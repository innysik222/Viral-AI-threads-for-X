### 🧠 Deep Dive
**Causal Models Matter for Accurate Predictions:**
Zera Therapeutics has shown that traditional descriptive data, while useful, is not sufficient for accurate predictions in complex biological systems. Their virtual cell model (XL) leverages causal data generated through high-throughput experimentation to predict gene expression changes more accurately than linear models.

**Outperformance of XL Model:**
Bo Wang and Chu from Zera Therapeutics describe a "wow moment" when their model predicted the response of unseen cell lines with unprecedented accuracy. This performance surpasses that of simpler, linear baselines, indicating the potential for significant advancements in AI-driven drug discovery.

**High-Throughput Experimentation:**
To generate the necessary causal data, Zera conducts extensive genetic perturbation experiments on various cell types. These experiments produce a massive dataset that is both diverse and rich, enabling their models to generalize effectively across different biological contexts.
