### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### The WSE-3 Chip: A Game-Changer in LLM Performance
Cerebras’ Wafer Scale Engine (WSE) is a revolutionary chip designed for fast inference. Unlike traditional chips that are cut into smaller pieces from a silicon wafer, the WSE keeps the wafer intact by routing around defective cores, thereby minimizing data movement and maximizing speed.

#### Impact on Agentic AI
Agentic workflows require extensive text generation, making them latency-sensitive. Fast inference enables these workflows to run in real time, generating hundreds of thousands of tokens before returning to users. This is a significant improvement over typical GPU setups where the bottleneck is often moving model weights from memory to compute units.

#### Simplified Application Development
With fast inference, developers can validate their applications between tasks rather than at the end. This means catching issues early and producing cleaner code, ultimately leading to more efficient development processes.
