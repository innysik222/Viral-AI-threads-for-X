### 🧠 Deep Dive
**The Shift to Specifying Problems**
Carina Hong, CEO of Axiom Math, explains that while AI can now handle complex mathematical problems with ease, accurately specifying these problems has become the new frontier. "We have reached a point where solving math problems is no longer the bottleneck," says Hong. "It's about translating real-world challenges into precise mathematical formulations."

Axiom Math observes that while players like Harmonic and Axiom are leading, fragmentation is increasing as other companies focus on specific areas. "People start their own initiatives without joining forces," Hong notes. "This creates a fragmented landscape where no one can fully capitalize on the advancements."
