### 🧠 Deep Dive
#### Democratizing AGI
Eiso Kant, founder of Poolside AI, believes that AGI should not be controlled by a few large companies. He argues that democratizing access to advanced AI technologies will lead to greater innovation and ethical development. "We want to support a world of abundance," Eiso states.

#### Model Factories
In an interview, Eiso discussed the importance of model factories in building efficient and scalable models. "Model building is ultimately 90% engineering," he said. The company focuses on creating a robust infrastructure that allows for continuous improvement and experimentation.

#### Open-Source Models
Eiso envisions a future where multiple players exist within the AGI landscape, each contributing to the development of intelligence. He argues that open-source models are essential in promoting competition and ensuring ethical use. "We want to be part of a world where companies, countries, and people can choose the provider they trust," Eiso said.

### 💬 Notable Quotes
> "We are going to be able to squeeze so much more out of smaller models than I think we had imagined in the industry because yes there's intelligence and larger models are more intelligent like no doubt about it." - Eiso Kant, Poolside AI

### 🏁 Conclusion
Eiso Kant’s journey from a failed $12M project to leading Poolside AI towards AGI is a testament to his vision. By promoting democratization, model factories, and open-source development, he aims to shape the future of artificial intelligence for the better.
