### Solving Pharma's Phase Three Failure Rate with Causal AI
**What the video argues:** BullFrog AI addresses the massive financial and temporal waste caused by high failure rates in late-stage clinical trials. By identifying specific biological drivers of complex diseases, the platform aims to move toward a precision medicine model where drug efficacy is predictable based on individual patient profiles.

**Key details:**
* Current industry standards involve a 50% failure rate in Phase III trials, despite drugs requiring over 10 years and $1–2 billion in investment to reach that stage.
* The BullFrog AI suite includes three core components: BF Prep (cleaning messy datasets), BF Leap (a causal AI engine for identifying drug targets), and BF Arenas (prioritizing the best options among potential targets).
* The technology specifically targets complex conditions like depression, bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia by pinpointing the specific genes and biological drivers behind them.

**Why it matters:** Reducing failure rates in Phase III doesn't just save billions of dollars; it drastically shortens the time-to-market for life-saving treatments by replacing "trial-and-error" drug development with data-driven certainty.
